## ğŸ” Business Rules

1. **Implementation Fee is One-Time**
   - Paid once per plan tier
   - Carries forward when upgrading
   - Only pay the difference

2. **Cannot Downgrade**
   - System only allows upgrades
   - Cannot go from Core â†’ Starter

3. **Billing Cycle Must Match**
   - Monthly plan can only upgrade to monthly plans
   - Yearly plan can only upgrade to yearly plans

4. **User Limit Enforcement**
   - Starter: 10 base, 20 max (with impl. fee)
   - Core: 100 users
   - Pro: 200 users
   - Elite: 500 users

5. **Overage Billing**
   - Only applies to Starter plan (11-20 users)
   - Rate: â‚±49/user/month
   - Other plans don't have overage, must upgrade
