# 13th Month Pay - Quick Reference Guide

## For HR Administrators

### Processing Steps (Quick)
1. **Select**: Choose "13th Month Pay" from Payroll Type
2. **Period**: Set From Year/Month and To Year/Month
3. **Date**: Select Payment Date
4. **Employees**: Select employees from list
5. **Process**: Click "Process Payroll" button

### Common Scenarios

| Scenario | From Period | To Period | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Full Year Annual | Jan 2024 | Dec 2024 | Standard annual processing |
| Mid-Year Payment | Jan 2024 | Jun 2024 | First half of year |
| Prorated (New Hire) | Apr 2024 | Dec 2024 | Employee joined April 1 |
| Multi-Year | Nov 2023 | Dec 2024 | Spans two calendar years |

### Formula Quick Reference
```
13th Month Pay = (Total Basic Pay + Leave Pay - Deductions) √∑ 12

Where Deductions include:
- Late Deductions
- Undertime Deductions
- Absent Deductions
```

## Common Issues & Quick Fixes

| Issue | Quick Fix |
|-------|-----------|
| **"No payroll records found"** | Check date range and ensure payrolls exist for that period |
| **"To date must be after From date"** | Verify year/month selections are in correct order |
| **Monthly breakdown not showing** | Refresh page or check if record is fully processed |
| **Incorrect amount** | View monthly breakdown to verify each month's calculation |
| **Can't see my payslip** | Ask HR to "Generate" the payslip (change status to Released) |

## Important Dates & Reminders

### Legal Requirements (Philippines)
- **Deadline**: On or before December 24 of each year
- **Coverage**: All rank-and-file employees
- **Basis**: Basic salary earned during the calendar year
